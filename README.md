# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition trends using Power BI and provides insights into workforce demographics and factors influencing employee turnover.

The dashboard is divided into two sections:

### Workforce Overview

* Total Employees
* Active Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Average Age
* Average Monthly Income
* Average Years at Company
* Department-wise Attrition
* Job Role-wise Attrition
* Employee Distribution by Age Group
* Employee Distribution by Gender
* Attrition by Education Field

### Attrition Drivers Analysis

* Over Time vs Attrition
* Job Satisfaction vs Attrition
* Work Life Balance vs Attrition
* Marital Status vs Attrition

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX
* Power Query
* Data Visualization

## Key Insights

* Research & Development department experienced the highest attrition.
* Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives showed the highest employee turnover.
* Employees working overtime exhibited higher attrition rates.
* Lower job satisfaction levels were associated with increased attrition.
* Single employees demonstrated higher attrition compared to married employees.
* Workforce distribution was concentrated in the 25–44 age group.
